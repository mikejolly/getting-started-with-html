Whitespace
less than, br, greater than = break (no closing tag (aka void tag)). Inline. Simply ends a line.
less than, p, greater than = paragraph (NOT a void tag, i.e. needs a closing tag. Block. Makes an invisible box around the text, has height, width, etc.

Span (inline)
Div (block) - these two relate to how text is laid out (CSS)
